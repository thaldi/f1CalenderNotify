Truth Lens is a media review app that helps users inspect photos, direct image links, and short videos with on-device signals. The app provides an estimate about whether content appears more like a real photo, an AI-generated image, an illustration, or a shared screen capture.

Privacy overview

Truth Lens is designed to keep review activity on the device whenever reasonably possible. Image and video analysis is primarily performed locally inside the app. Optional feedback sharing and cloud sync features are disabled by default and require explicit user action.

  • Core image analysis is performed locally on-device.
  • Video analysis samples frames locally on-device.
  • Optional feedback uploads are opt-in and may include selected media plus review metadata.
  • Cloud-synced feedback is only sent after explicit consent.
  • Users can turn consent off and remove local feedback data from the app.

Data use and third parties

The app may use Firebase services for optional feedback upload and remote configuration, Google Mobile Ads for ad delivery, and in-app purchase providers for subscriptions or credit packs. These services operate under their own policies in addition to the app's own product behavior.

  • Firebase: remote config, optional feedback sync, and related cloud storage.
  • Google Mobile Ads: ad serving and measurement.
  • Store providers: purchase validation and pricing for remove-ads or credit products.

Retention summary

  • Local-only analysis inputs stay on the device unless the user explicitly shares them.
  • Optional feedback items may remain stored locally until deleted or synced.
  • Cloud feedback may remain in the project backend for product-improvement workflows.
  • Store and ad providers may process transactional or diagnostic metadata under their own terms.

Terms of use summary

By using Truth Lens, users acknowledge that outputs are informational estimates and not a guarantee of authenticity, legality, safety, or ownership. Users remain responsible for how they interpret and act on app results.

  • The service is provided on an "as available" basis.
  • Outputs should not be relied on as legal, forensic, or moderation evidence.
  • Users are responsible for ensuring they have rights to media they submit or share.
  • App features, pricing, and availability may change over time.

Product notes

  • Results are estimates and should be interpreted with caution.
  • The app may show "real" while also indicating that content looks like a screenshot or shared capture.
  • Stylized, illustrated, or painterly content can be difficult and may still be misread.
  • Short videos are analyzed using sampled frames and video-level aggregation.
  • Some premium or credit-based features may vary by store region and product configuration.
